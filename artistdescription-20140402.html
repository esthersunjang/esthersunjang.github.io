Interesting things about Andrea del Sarto:
Andrea del Sarto was considered one of the great artists of his time, along with da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Raphael.

Del Sarto often modeled the Virgin Mary in his paintings after his wife, Lucrezia. However, del Sarto's pupil described Lucrezia as "faithless, jealous, and vixenish with the apprentices."

Del Sarto allegedly ruined his reputation by stealing from the King of France, François I. The King invited del Sarto to France and gave him a sum of money with which he was to purchase works of art for the French Court. Instead, del Sarto allegedly bought himself a house in Florence after which he was forbidden from returning to France.
