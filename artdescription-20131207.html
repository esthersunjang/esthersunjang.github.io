Time Will Tell, by Leah Rosenberg.
2010.
Cake.

This cake is the edible form of Leah's "Time Will Tell" series paintings. Each stripe represents memories and experiences (color from friend's clothes, meals, conversations, etc.) The series as a whole represents the infinite number of possibilities that exists within a fixed form.
