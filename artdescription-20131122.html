Elephant, by Rembrandt van Rijn.
1637.
Black chalk on paper.
Current Location: Albertina, Vienna.

In 1637, Rembrandt drew several portraits of Hansken, the performing elephant. Hansken, in addition to being a famous performing elephant at the time, has since become known for being the first to define her species, Elephas maximus (Asian elephant). I like this portrait because it is quite different from the works for which Rembrandt is known. (The Nightwatch, Abduction of Europa, etc.)
I also just love elephants.
