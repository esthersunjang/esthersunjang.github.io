Interesting things about William Beall:
William Beall was a Marine combat photographer who was on Iwo Jima on the same day that 5 American soldiers raised a U.S. flag atop Mount Suribachi. However, he was on the wrong side of the island and his fellow photographer Joe Rosenthal ended up snapping the historic image. It wasn't until 12 years later that Beall managed to snap his Pulitzer winning photograph, Faith and Confidence.
