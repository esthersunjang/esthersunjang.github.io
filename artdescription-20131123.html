Mockingbirds, by Christina Empedocles.
2013.
Wax pencil on paper.
Current Location: San Francisco, USA

Christina initially created photocopied pieces of actual birds for a show in D.C. when the curator asked if she could create drawings instead. Since then, Christina has been creating these lovely drawings of birds on paper. Check out more of her work here.
